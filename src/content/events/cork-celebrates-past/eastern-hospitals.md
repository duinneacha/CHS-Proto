<p class="indentedText">
        As part of weekend of activities to commemorate Cork Celebrates Past Exhibition, 
        Paul Shaw, Archivist for the Poor Servants of the Mother of God, Brentford, accepted
        our invitation to give a presentation on a little-known publication, Eastern Hospitals
        & English Nurses, by Fanny Taylor as she was then known. It is an account of her time 
        as a volunteer nurse for Florence Nightingale during the Crimean War.
    </p>
    <p class="indentedText">
        The presentation took place, 29<sup>th</sup> September 2023, at Carrigtwohill Community 
        Centre, which was very well attended.
    </p>
    <P class="indentedText">
        The video is approximately 78 minutes duration. Unfortunately, the lighting and camera 
        angle are not great.
    </P>
