<p class="indentedText">
        Dr Seán McCarthy, from Drinagh in West Cork (now living in Glenville) as a young PhD student in
        UCC in 1984, put Fota Island, Carrigtwohill on the map in the heart of the EEC in Brussels. His
        project was on the study of Solar PV panels and their potential use. This was one of the main 
        energy research projects funded by the EEC in that period. Solar power, which was in its infancy
        at the time, had never been tested in Ireland, apart from providing power to Charlie Haughey’s 
        house on Inishvickillane in the Blaskets.
    </p>
