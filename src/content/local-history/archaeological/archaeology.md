<p class="indentedText">
        Carrigtwohill and its surrounding district is festooned with sites of significant archaeological
        importance ranging from local middens, ringforts, Wells (Holy Wells), and ancient structural ruins.
    </p>
    <p class="indentedText">
        Under section 2.2 of our society’s constitution, it is our duty to “To preserve and protect all 
        our remaining historical features.”
    </p>
