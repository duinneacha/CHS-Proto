<h1>Local History</h1>
    <h2>Ballyadam Estate</h2>
    <h3>Save Ballyadam Bridge</h3>
    <p class="indentedText">
        We have recently been advised by Alan O’Connor, a longstanding member of our society, of
        a plan to develop the railway line between Glounthaune and Midleton including twin tracking
        of the line. Their plan incorporates the demolition of the bridge at Ballyadam House. 
    </p>
    <p class="indentedText">
        If you would like to learn more about our petition submission objecting to the proposed 
        demolition, please click link to read article of our <a href="/local-history/ballyadam/petition-elementpdf/" target="_blank">Christmas General Circular</a>. 
        If you agree with what we are hoping to achieve, you signature would be most welcome.
        </p>
    <p class="indentedText">
        This signature application will be stopped, 11<sup>th</sup> January 2023, to enable the collection
        of signatories, and to ensure no duplications. The submission date is the following day.
    </p>
