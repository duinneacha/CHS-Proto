Killacloyne, Church of the Sequestered Place, the church which gave the townland its name was found to be at the Northern end. The exact spot was found apparently with some difficulty on Fentons farm, on the western side of the road heading towards Liberty Hall, Foundations of a building presumable an early church are faintly traceable about 18 feet by 10. Part of the townland of Killacloyne was in the neighbouring parish of Caherlag
